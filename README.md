**Bold** is a decentralized prediction market platform where you can bet on the outcomes of future events. From politics and sports to technology and finance, if an event has a verifiable outcome, you can create a market for it on Bold.

## What is a Prediction Market?

Prediction markets, also known as information markets or idea futures, are exchange-traded markets created for the purpose of trading on the outcomes of events. The market prices can indicate what the crowd believes the probability of the event is. Bold brings this powerful concept to the high-speed, low-cost Solana blockchain, making it accessible to everyone.

By allowing users to buy and sell "shares" in the outcome of an event, Bold aggregates collective knowledge and provides a real-time forecast. For example, in a market asking "Will Team A win the championship?", the price of a "YES" share can be interpreted as the market's estimated probability of that outcome.

## The Vision for Bold

Bold aims to be more than just a betting platform; it's a tool for aggregating collective intelligence and discovering the truth. By incentivizing participants to be well-informed and make accurate predictions, prediction markets can often produce forecasts that are more reliable than individual experts.

-   **Hedge against real-world risks.**
-   **Discover the true probability of important future events.**
-   **Engage in intellectually stimulating discussions and debates.**
-   **Monetize their knowledge and research.**
